Dona Kim, a recent Harvard graduate and Houstonian, is spearheading an HIV/AIDS youth education project called Grades4AIDS. The project aims to launch in H.I.S.D. during the 2002-2003 academic year and seeks support from Harvard alumni in Houston through contributions of time and/or money. Dona Kim is currently forming an Advisory Board and Board of Trustees and is fundraising for project operational costs until May 2003. More information is available on their website www.grades4aids.org or by contacting Dona Kim directly.
