Jaclyn Grahmann is organizing a Summer Volleyball league at Third Coast Volleyball. Interested participants should email Jaclyn by Wednesday, June 13 with their preferred night (Wednesday or Thursday), level (Recreational or Intermediate), team members, and captain information. If they don't have a team, Jaclyn will try to organize one. Games start on Wednesday, June 20 and will be played on Wednesday or Thursday evenings between 6:30 pm and 9:30 pm. The season will be 7 weeks long with playoffs.
