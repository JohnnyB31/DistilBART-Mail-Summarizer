The Indian Prime Minister will be in Washington on November 7, with a meeting planned for November 8 with the Secretary of the PMO. John Hardy is seeking input on who should attend this meeting. The State Department has provided information that UnderSecretary Alan Larson met with Indian officials and received a commitment to energize the government towards a solution for Dabhol before the PM arrives. The US-India Business Council is also planning a letter to the PM, emphasizing the urgency of negotiations and the need for a meeting schedule.
