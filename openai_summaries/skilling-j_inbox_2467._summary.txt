Dona Kim, a recent Harvard graduate, is developing the Grades4AIDS project to promote HIV/AIDS education and youth leadership in Houston, seeking support from Harvard alumni. Jim Hughes, on behalf of the Harvard Club, highlights this initiative to encourage community involvement. Dona is seeking contributions of time and money, and is working on forming an advisory board. She aims to launch the project during the 2002-2003 school year and is actively putting together the necessary organizational structure.
