PJM West market trials for the Day Ahead Market will occur on November 20-21, with participants encouraged to submit and modify bids and offers via the specified website. The market opens at 8:00 a.m. on November 20 and closes at 12:00 p.m. the same day; results will be posted by 12:00 p.m. on November 21. Another trial is scheduled for November 27. Participants are instructed to login and submit data, and can view results the following day.
