Wabash Power, GPU Energy, and Ameren have all sent termination notices for their wholesale power transactions with Enron, with effective dates around December 19-21, 2001. Wabash's termination covers all transactions; GPU's termination is being finalized, and Ameren's notice is under verification. Edward Baughman is confirming if there are any one-off contracts and whether Wabash's notice covers them.
