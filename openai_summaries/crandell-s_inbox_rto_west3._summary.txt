The emails discuss comments on RTO West's congestion management and access charge proposals. A coalition of generation interests filed comments criticizing the current proposal for discriminating against nonincumbents and providing few incentives for existing contract holders to convert. CFACTS, a coalition committed to developing a fair market structure, also submitted comments supporting non-discriminatory access and open competition. The RTO West is preparing a March 1, 2002 Stage "2" FERC filing.
