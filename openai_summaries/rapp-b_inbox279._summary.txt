Mary Kay Miller reports that a protest and request for a Tech Conference or hearing regarding the USGT transaction, which involves a volume of 400,000 per day, has been received from the CPUC starting Nov 1, 2001. She mentions that IS is expected to file a protest as well. Kimberly Watson asks about the usual process, response timelines, and FERC decision schedule. Mary Kay indicates they will prepare an answer to the protests, with the FERC expected to issue an order by the end of November.
